Othello

Othello is the play's protagonist and hero. He is a Christian Moor and general of the armies of Venice. He is eloquent and physically powerful figure, respected by all of those around him. In spite of his elevated status, he is insecure because of his age, his life as a soldier and his race. He has a "free and open nature"which his ensign, Iago, uses to twist his love for his wife, Desdemona, into a powerful and destructive jealousy.
